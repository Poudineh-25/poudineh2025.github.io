About Me

My name is Mohammadreza Poudineh, a graduate student in Graph Theory and Combinatorics. My world exists at the fascinating crossroads of Mathematics, Art, and Computer Science. I am driven by a passion to uncover the hidden patterns that connect abstract ideas to tangible creations. Whether I am deconstructing a mathematical theorem, crafting a piece of calligraphy, or writing Python code, I am exploring different languages of structure and form.

Beyond research, I teach Mathematics, believing it to be a creative language to be understood, not just memorized. As a calligrapher, I blend precision with emotion, and as a budding programmer, I use Python to bring my ideas to life. This website showcases my journey and interdisciplinary pursuits.
